About Us





Doing good so that others can do better

We measure our own success by a sole yardstick: what impact have we made. Equiglobe India is founded by a group of senior knowledge professionals with exceptional backgrounds across diversified sectors. All the board members are passionate about creating an impact for the betterment of society. Each of the founders has a history of ‘doing good’ and creating impact The organization is led by passionate professionals.


Vision

To empower people, inspire action for sustainable, equitable and inclusive world.


Mission

We dedicate our lives to support an enabling ecosystem, and deliver innovative and comprehensive solutions for an equable society.
• Enhance people capability for change and action
• Providing an enabling ecosystem for the less privileged to grow and develop to their fullest potential
• Enhance Equity, provide dignity through financial inclusion
• Work for the success of every stakeholder. Everyone has the potential to succeed
• Work for the growth of the society through participative action
